Introduction

This idea is a mobile app solution designed to support better freediving and spearfishing safety and educate them about ocean wildlife in the island of Cebu, Philippines.

at least 10 freedivers die per month in Cebu

The solution was inspired to prevent and lessen dive casualties through information and also by providing divers a platform to meet potential dive buddies.
