Goals

Introduction

This idea is a mobile app solution aimed to support better freediving and spearfishing safety and educate divers about ocean wildlife in the island of Cebu, Philippines.

at least 10 freedivers die per month in Cebu

The solution was inspired to prevent and lessen dive casualties through information and also by providing divers a platform to meet potential dive buddies who are on the same level as them.

This app is user friendly to everyone
  • UI/UX will be user friendly for both new and old users
  • Majority can't speak english so this app is available in 2 languages
This app offers local ocean wildlife info
  • This app has information on local ocean wildlife by type
  • The app displays all possible local species of that type
  • This app will give specific details about a fish
    • Is this edible?
    • is this poisonous/deadly?
    • Remedies if poisoned
    • Is this endangered? -- allows spearfishers to avoid hunting this fish
This app offers a wide audience
  • Everyone who is interested in ocean wildlife can utilize this app
    • Both professional and amateur freedivers can use it
    • A non-diver beachgoer can use it
    • An aquatic pet owner can use it
This app is a dedicated platform for diving to find and chat other divers

The number 1 rule in diving is "Never Dive Alone" but the majority of divers here go solo since the local diving community is small. It is also not easy to chat strangers on social media apps so it's hard to find dive buddies for safety

  • This app allows users to make an account
  • This app allows users to chat with other users
  • This app allows users to setup their profile

Amatueur divers can't dive as deep as experienced divers, this means that they can't save them if anything happens in deep waters. This app's user profiles can show important personal dive info so that other divers can know if they are a potential dive buddy

  • Their profile displays depth - how deep can they dive
  • Also shows what level of certification they have
This app displays info for popular local beaches/resort
  • Risks in the beach and some advice
    • A lof of sea urchins here so be careful
    • A lot of jellyfish here so wear long sleeves and long pants
    • Shark infested waters, beware
    • The underwater caves here have high currents! Stay away
  • Possible ocean wildlife sightings (by type) and rules
    • A lof of turtles here! But never touch them they can drown
  • Local beach/resort rules
    • No spearfishing allowed here
    • Don't touch the coral nursery
    • You're not allowed in the water without a dive buddy
  • Dive Spots
    • Underwater plane wreck at 20meters
    • Underwater statue of Jesus at 12meters
This app allows uploading of images and videos
