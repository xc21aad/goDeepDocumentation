Persona 1

Ben Mercado

  • Experience level: Professional
  • Depth: 30meters
  • Age: 33

Ben is a professional spearfisher. He spearfishes everyday by freediving which means diving with one breath and no oxygen tank, and sells what he catches because that is his daily job. Ben solo dives a lot through because he doesn't know anyone else who can dive with him and also as deep as him. But to earn money he still needs to catch fish so he still goes spearfishing alone despite the dangers of diving deep waters with just one breath.

Ben wants to:

  1. Open and load the app on his phone
  2. Press profile icon
  3. Press make an account
  4. Enters his details
  5. Press complete
  6. Press fish icon
  7. Inputs jellyfish
  8. Compares jellyfish species photos to what he saw on water
  9. Press on box jellyfish and reads info
  10. Press friends icon to find a dive buddy
  11. Press on a user's profile and check dive data
  12. Press send message button
  13. Inputs chat
  14. Press send
