Critique

Dive Plus

Dive Plus app is the only dive app on both App Store and Play Store. It is similar to my app which focuses on the diving community.

Advantages

  • The app is available in multiple languages
  • You can edit underwater photos/videos for free but with watermark
  • Focuses on community because scuba divers should not dive alone too
  • You can post photos and videos and leave bubbles (likes)

Disadvantages

  • Its a scuba app, Im looking for a freediving app
  • It doesnt let me find out what fish I am looking for
  • There is a dive spot info but it is too vague because it only shows regions/states. I want more specific like beaches and resorts.
  • Not enough information on the dive spots. Doesnt tell me what wildlife I can see, what risks are there and rules of the dive spot
